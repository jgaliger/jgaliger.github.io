So... What is rock climbing?

There are two types of rock climbing:

  • Bouldering
  • Belaying

Bouldering is a lot simpler and safer than belaying, and often is the choice of climb for people that are afraid of heights. The walls are a lot shorter, and often dont go above 20-25 feet. Not only this, but there are very thick mats under you to break your fall as well.

Belaying is a lot more climbing, but requires technique and a lot of energy. Instead of 20-25 feet, these walls can often strech up to 100-150 instead. However, there are often auto-belays at rock climbing gyms where you can climb as high as you want without worry.

Where can I go?

Actually, there are rock climbing gyms all over the place! Down here in charlotte, I go to Inner Peaks, which has a large variety of bouldering. There often is a gym very close by, you just have to find it!

Not only this, but once you become good enough you could even start to climb rocks just out in the wild! This of course would be very far off in the future, but it is definitely something to look forward to!

Why Should You Climb?

In my opinion, rock climbing is something most people can get into! Since it involves your body weight, people often start off on the same level and have lots of room for growth. The only issue is in the beginning, it is very hard on your fingers if you don't already have callouses. However, with chalk, it makes it a lot easier on yourself!
