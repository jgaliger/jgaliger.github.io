ITIS 3135 Website Evaluations

First Website

This website is very interesting as it seems like it was made as a joke website, however, it follows the CRAP principals really well. Within the webiste, I noticed that they had extremely good contrast and also proximity.

  • Contrast: The contrast they used in this website was not just white and black text. There was actually quite a few changes in background color, with cool patters along with it.
  • Repetition: Repetition was really good as with the items being sold, each item was portrayed in a very similar manner.
  • Alignment: The alignment swithced up quite a bit, but most pictures and texts were very well aligned.
  • Proximity: Every picture was very closely related, and they were grouped together by subject

Second Website

This website was pretty terrible. There seemed to be no contrast, there was just a simple set of numbers on the screen that changed quickly. There was not a lot of content.

  • Contrast: No contrast, the only colors there were white and black.
  • Repetition: There was repetition, the sounds that the binary piano repeated until it got tiring very quickly
  • Alignment: There was alignment, the binary numbers were centered.
  • Proximity: There was not enough content for proximity.
